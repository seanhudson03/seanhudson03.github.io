About Sean Hudson

Sean Hudson is a second year computer science student studying at
the University of Rhode Island. Sean started as a business major,
but soon realized he would much rather switch into a STEM field,
because that's what he was used to. Programming aside, Sean enjoys
spending time with his friends and girlfriend. In between semesters
Sean works as a part-time bartender, and as the Head Sailing
Instructor at the Keyport Yacht Club Junior Sailing Program,
which he has been involved with since he was 7.
