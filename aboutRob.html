About Robert Vets III

With every step into nature and every tree passed, feelings of childhood wonder and awe flood my mind and cease all thoughts of worry and angst. I cannot help but to be happy in the presence of the birds, the bees, and everything in between. Exhibiting the beauty of the natural world and all that it is home to - through photography - has become my life’s goal. No matter where you look, there will always be some sparkle of life to be seen - and no matter how far the hike, cold the temperature, rain or shine, I will find it.
- Robbie
