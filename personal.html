URI: THE BIRDS THE BEES AND EVERYTHING IN BETWEEN

Life is such a beautiful thing and it comes in so many shapes and sizes. URI’s campus is the home to many different varieties of life from cows to squirrels to koi. The goal of my project was to show all these wonderful creatures as I found them. Some of them roam the paths alongside our classrooms and residence halls, while others live on the farm or in the wooded areas around campus. No matter where you look, there will always be some sparkle of life to be seen. If we stop for a moment and really pay attention to these amazing beings, we’ll start to notice we aren’t so different from them. Whether it be a smiling goat or a hungry squirrel, there is always a little bit of humanity to be found in each and every living thing.
